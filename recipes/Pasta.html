How to make PASTA from scratch

Discription

Pasta is a staple Italian dish made from durum wheat
flour and water, often shaped into various forms like
spaghetti or penne. It's cooked by boiling and enjoyed
with a wide range of sauces, proteins, and vegetables.
With its versatility and rich history, pasta has become
a beloved comfort food enjoyed worldwide.

Steps

Ingredients

8 ounces (about 225 grams) of pasta (spaghetti, penne, or any type you prefer)
4 cloves of garlic, minced
4 tablespoons of butter
1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and black pepper to taste
Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)


Instructions

Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a
Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions
until al dente. Drain the pasta, reserving about 1/2 cup of
the pasta water. Prepare the Garlic Butter Sauce: In a large skillet,melt the
butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for
1-2 minutes until fragrant. Be careful not to let the garlic
burn. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet
with the garlic butter sauce. Toss well to coat the pasta
evenly with the sauce.
Add Cheese: Gradually sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese
over the pasta, tossing continuously until the cheese is melted
and forms a creamy sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, you can
add some of the reserved pasta water to thin it out.
Season and Garnish: Season the pasta with salt and black pepper
to taste. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley or basil if desired.
Serve: Transfer the garlic and cheese pasta to serving plates
or bowls. You can sprinkle extra Parmesan cheese on top if you
like. Serve hot and enjoy!
This recipe is quick, easy, and bursting with flavor. Feel free
to customize it by adding cooked vegetables, grilled chicken,
or crispy bacon for extra deliciousness!
